# Pantry Staples

Long‑term dry goods that should be replenished when running low.

- Baking supplies (except sugar)
- Beans and lentils
- Dried peas
- Assorted sauces (soy, teriyaki, etc.)
- Rice (various types)
- Pasta (various shapes)
- Chopped tomatoes (canned)
- Coconut milk
- Salt, pepper, spices
- Oil (cooking oil, olive oil)

Note: Everyday items like bread, eggs, milk, butter, onions, garlic are managed separately by the user.
